About Us

```

Neon Sakura is an anime-inspired virtual band formed in a digital world. The band consists of four members, each representing a different emotion: love, chaos, memory, and control. Their music blends electronic beats with orchestral sounds, creating a unique experience that feels both futuristic and emotional.

The band became popular through online performances in a simulated city. Fans are drawn to their visual identity, glowing colors, and immersive storytelling. Each song tells a story about identity, memory, and connection.
